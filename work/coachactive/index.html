The Process

When beginning work on this concept I immediately took note of a comment made by the client to avoid using the image of a football as this may appear as too generic. I decided, in that case, to focus in on the word RECRUITS and work from that angle with the image of a player. This can be a little tricky too, as many sports-related logos and emblems feature figures running or passing a football. Ultimately I wanted to acheive a visual representation of the words 'recruit', 'sport' and 'AFL', tying them together without cluttering the concept or appearing too generic.

Layout/Application

Ensuring that the logo is suited to a number of different applications is an important consideration when developing a concept. Where and how the logo is seen may not always be ideal, but strong, well-balanced visual elements should always successfully deliver your visual message, even when resized or rearranged.

Colours/Font

Previous concepts had used red as the primary colour and so I have continued with this. Although this concept lends itself well to other colours I think the combination of red and dark grey tones creates a strong visual impact, and successfully communicates the brand purpose and function.
