AFA Terms & Conditions

Payment

Fees are payable in advance. It is a basic condition of attendance that fees are payable during the registration day.

Players should not attend future classes if fees are not paid, unless prior agreement with an Advanced Football Academy staff member has been made.

Dress Code

Please send your child to training sessions with the standard football gear, with appropriate footwear: football boots, football shin pads, soccer ball and water bottle. Joggers and snickers are not allowed and shin pads must be worn at all times for the protection of players. There is a “no shin pad no play” policy in place.

Jewelry CANNOT be worn as they may be torn from the body causing physical damage and/or may be lost. Advanced Football Academy takes no responsibility for personal items which may be lost and/or damaged.

Note: During Summer Months, as the temperature rises we kindly ask that all participations wear a hat and sunscreen.

Attendance

Please ensure your child is punctual in his/her attendance at all training classes (at least 10mins prior to the start time). If your child is late he/she may be asked to sit at the side of the session until the trainer/group has finished their current practice.

Please ensure an Advanced Football Academy staff member is present at the venue before leaving your child for his/her class.

During Class

Parents/Guardians and siblings are NOT to enter the field of play at ANY time during the class. Please keep to a safe distance away from the training area. Persistent offenders may be asked to leave the site and their child may be asked to leave the Sessions without a refund.

Personal Behavior

The player must respect the trainer and other attendees in the class. In the case of bad language, violence, ignorant conduct and behavior that prevents the class from proceeding, the trainer and/or Advanced Football Academy staff reserve the right to remove the player from the class. If the player does not comply, the trainer may, in his/her discretion, terminate the player’s training class.

Advanced Football Academy may change the services offered from time to time. Changes apply immediately on notification to you.

Collection

Your child must be collected from the playing area. If you wish for your child to be collected by a different person, please inform Advanced Football Academy staff of who that person shall be prior to the session beginning either via telephone or SMS/ email notification.

If your child is collected more than 15 minutes late on several occasions, he/she may be asked to leave the Training Sessions – No refund shall be given.

Illness

If your child is ill and cannot attend a class, Advanced Football Academy staff and coaches must be notified as soon as possible via SMS/Email before the session starts. Advanced Football Academy will attempt to provide make up classes on a predefined venue/day. However, this will only be done on a best effort basis.

Refunds

Refunds shall not be given if a class is cancelled outside of Advanced Football Academy’s control – e.g. Extreme weather. Refunds shall not be given to those people who choose to go on holiday during term time.
