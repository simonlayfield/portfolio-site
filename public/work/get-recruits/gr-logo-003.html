The Process

The aim of this concept is to purposefully disconnect the typeface with a visual icon (such as a player or football as used previously). I have presented a version below that unifies the typeface and he image of a football (for reasons of clarity) but the focus of the concept was to allow each component to be used together or separately and where necessary in the theme of the website and other collateral.

Layout/Application

Ensuring that the logo is suited to a number of different applications is an important consideration when developing a concept. Where and how the logo is seen may not always be ideal, but strong, well-balanced visual elements should always successfully deliver your visual message, even when resized or rearranged.
